# Welcome to WordMD

WordMD is a friendly, **Word-like** Markdown editor for Windows.

## What it can do

- **Bold**, *italic*, ~~strikethrough~~, and `inline code`
- Headings 1 through 6
- Bullet lists, numbered lists, and task lists
- Blockquotes and `code blocks`
- Tables, links, and images

> The doctor is in. *Markdown made painless.*
